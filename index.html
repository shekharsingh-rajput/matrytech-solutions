IT staff augmentation is a flexible outsourcing strategy that enables you to hire tech talent globally and manage your augmented team directly. It allows you to fill skill gaps in your in-house team with external specialists for a specific project or period.

Staff augmentation works by providing you with skilled professionals who integrate seamlessly with your existing team. You maintain full control over the project while we handle recruitment, vetting, and administrative tasks.

Benefits include cost-effectiveness, access to global talent, scalability, reduced hiring time, and the ability to maintain control over your projects while accessing specialized skills.

The hourly rates are calculated individually for each project, as they’re based on the clients’ objectives and needs, the average price tag varies from $50 to $99 per hour. Nevertheless, compared to its CEE competitors, the Matrytech team features really attractive rates for the services delivered.

IT staff augmentation model assumes the only service commitment is hours of work. The outsourcing model (or managed services) assumes that the supplier assumes all the risks of meeting the service commitment..
